Edit Cardios

2 things here: first is just a verb you associate with equipment. Cardio is randomly selected and when you tell a person at a personal level to go bike or run or whatever, the pre-condition is that they have the associated equipment

2nd half is way more complicated but shouldn't need any modifying at this point. Workout generator assigns low, medium, or heavy cardio, and from there either a zone 1, 2, or 3 type workout is output, and that part is random. Zone 2 and 3 workouts both drop down to the previous zone.

If you also see the hardcoded rules, this forces the program to point to a cardio type even if a person is really in shape or whatever. For example, a really in shape bodybuilder or powerlifter is not going to do intense or overly long cardio, so we would point those guys to always go to the beginner cardio.

Also, if you leave any case unaccounted for as far as a fitness level or phase, that will also break things
